What is Chordify?

Chordify is a tool that lets musicians identify chords from a list of notes.

How to use Chordify?

Enter in your notes on the virtual piano and press the Search button. Below the piano, it will show a list of chords that contain your notes. The first chord in the list is the most well-known or “common” chord that has those notes.
